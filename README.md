#  Simbas Friends - Find Playdates for Your Dog!

##  About the App

**Simbas Friends** is a social networking app designed for **dog owners** to **find friends for their dogs**, schedule **meetups**, and **join local events**. Whether you're new in town or just looking to expand your dog's social circle, this app makes it **easy to connect with other dog owners nearby**.

---

##  Features

###  User Profiles
锔 Each user has a **profile** where they can:
- Upload photos of their dog  
- Edit their **bio** and **profile picture**  
- View and manage their **friend list**  

###  Event Map & Listings
锔 The **main page** is a **map** displaying all **nearby dog events (within 20 km)**  
锔 Tap an event in the list and the map will **highlight its location**  
锔 Event details include **location**, **time**, **description**, and **participants**

###  Create & Join Events
锔 Users can **create events** by:
- **Using current location** or **typing an address**  
- **Previewing the location** on the map before confirming  
锔 Users can **join events** and view participants before attending  
锔 Filter your events to see **upcoming** or **past** meetups

###  Friends & Chat
锔 Send and respond to **friend requests**  
锔 **One-on-one chat** to plan playdates  

###  Gallery & Image Uploads
锔 Upload and delete multiple **dog photos**  
锔 **Lottie animations** display during uploads for smoother UX  

###  Persistent Login
锔 Stay **logged in** until you choose to log out  

###  Smooth User Experience
锔 **Loading animations** for long-running actions  
锔 **Auto-scrolling chat** to keep the latest messages in view  
锔 **Form resets** prevent duplicate submissions  

---

##  Tech Stack
- **Language:** Kotlin  
- **Database:** Firebase Firestore  
- **Authentication:** Firebase Auth  
- **Storage:** Firebase Storage  
- **Maps:** Google Maps API  
- **UI:** Bottom Navigation 路 RecyclerView 路 Lottie 路 Glide 路 ImagePicker
